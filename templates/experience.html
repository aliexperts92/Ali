{{ site.name }} opened in {{ site.est }} on the grounds of La Cantera Resort, envisioned as a fine-dining experience that rivals a one-Michelin-star restaurant — yet remains unmistakably Texan. The cuisine is rooted in French tradition and enlivened by South Texas and Hill Country spirit: Gulf seafood, ranch-raised proteins, and produce from the restaurant's own kitchen garden.

The vision traces to James Beard-nominated chef Andrew Weissman, founder of San Antonio's legendary Le Rêve and our consulting founding chef.
